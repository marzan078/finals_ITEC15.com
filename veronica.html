Establishments

If you're seeking for a quiet area to unwind and relax away from the hustle and bustle of the city... Then Villa Veronica Tagaytay is the ideal location for you.




ENJOY THE STAY IN VILLA VERONICA


Villa Veronica Tagaytay was converted from a family resthouse into a private resort for small parties and celebrations. It is located in Brgy Bucal Silang Cavite, close to Anya Residences, Asiong's, and Chateau Jestia, yet has Tagaytay weather. Tagaytay proper is about a 5-minute drive away.
